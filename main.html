Interstellar

Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction movie about a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity. It explores themes of time, love, and survival in space.

Actors

The main actors include Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, and Michael Caine. Their performances bring emotional depth and realism to the story.

Favourite Quote
